NLP w praktyce

Amanda Vickers, Steve Bavister

Probably the only book you will ever need. It introduces you in to the world of NLP which stands for Neuro Linguistic Programming. You will learn how to use your brain in more extensible way.

And below I would like to present reviewers my opinion about book


The book in very easy way explains what NLP is and and why it is today one of the most efficient tools of personal and career development. By reading that book you can learn most popular techniques and starategies, which allows you efficiently manage your mind and emotions, reach your targets and steer your career. Together with theory you will find a lot of practical examples and excercises.
